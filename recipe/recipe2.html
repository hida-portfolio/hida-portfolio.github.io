Moat Lin Mayar ( Couple Snack)

History

The meaning of mont lin ma yar means husband and wife snack. The reason behind of this snack call is two pieces combine into one which represent two people get married and they become into one life.It is suitable for any religious because it purely make by rice.

Moat Lin Mayar is a tiny cripy and round pankcake. Made with a mixture of rice flour and chickpeas. It is also topped with quial egg, spring onion, and white sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • Fine Rice Flour : 150g
  • Water : 350g
  • Tempura : 50g
  • Chickpea Powder : 2 Tbsp
  • Burmese yellow sprouted peas : 60g
  • Salt : 1/2 Tsp
  • spring onion : 2
  • Olive Oil : 4 Tbsp
  • Sesame Seeds : 2 Tbsp
  • Chive : Garnish
  • Quail egg : 15ish

Direction

  1. 1 hour ahead, mix rice flour, tempura, chickpea powder, Â¼ Teaspoon of salt and water , set aside.
  2. Preparation, chop spring onions. Get your molded pan, chopstick, long handle spoon, brush ready
  3. Heat the pan evenly, brush with oil, pour the batter into individual mold just over half but not full.
  4. Top up with quial egg, and/or burmese yellow sprouted peas, and chopped spring onions.
    Cover it up for 1 minute. When it gets crispy outside, scoop one and close it to another one
    like a lid. Brush with oil regularly to make sure it cooks evenly.
    Once it all cooked both sides and inside, you can place them onto plate.

Tips

Use small molded pan.

Cook slowly to cook the egg properly

make sure the pan is hot when you put the batter in
